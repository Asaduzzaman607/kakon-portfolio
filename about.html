About Me

As a fashion admirer, I love to explore all kinds of design patterns, materials and garments from different walks of life. Intensely observing the details, I play with ancient silhouettes, shapes, colours, and embroideries. Using comfortable fabrics and techniques such as hand embroidery, and hand stitching with beads or sequins I enjoy giving the designs a unique personal touch and remarks.


For me, fashion is not just looking glamorous but being yourself, showing personality, and finding comfort. Creating new designs with an intertwin relation with antique patterns inspires me immensely. Moreover, preserving ancient techniques and crafts is incredibly important as well in my view.
