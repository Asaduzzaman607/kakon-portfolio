PartyWear

Collection 2021

Inspired by Ruby gemstones I adore and the dresses that have Gemstones in them. So, I have designed a low backless ...long party dress that has a few red chains with jewels in it. Moreover, I gave a long slit on one side of the dress and a red jewel work all over it.

Casual Wear

Collection 2022

As a result of some latest TV shows, corset tops are now super trendy and they are my favorite for that snatched look. So, I wanted to make them look comfortable, and regular wear because corsets are pretty uncomfortable. For that, I mixed it up with puffy sleeves and buttons on the front instead of tight laces and a deep-cut V-neckline. I paired it with a loose-fitted pencil skirt

Butter

fly

Collection 2022

I was inspired by the Starry Night Cracker Butterfly. It has very sophisticated colors. I wanted to make it simple. So, I kept the body part simple but gave it a deep-cut neck with bullet-shaped breast cups. The cup has pipe-work in it. Also, a detachable puffy balloon long trains to replicate the wings. The skirt is attached to a belt.

SportsWear

Collection 2022

Comfortable sports wear is hard to find in our country. So I designed a super stretchy legging with a v-shaped elastic-free waist to give a stylish look and jersey rib knit fabric for the meshy details. The top is also designed to provide support during workouts. I kept the strips pretty wide so it doesn't pinch our shoulders. Besides, I have designed a big oversized matching jacket with puffy sleeves for after workouts that can be worn as a casual jacket too.
